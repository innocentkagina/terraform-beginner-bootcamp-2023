The Flintstones

Fred Flintstone

Fred Flintstone is the main character of the Flintstones TV show. He is a hardworking blue-collar worker who loves bowling and spending time with his family.

Wilma Flintstone

Wilma Flintstone is Fred's wife and mother of Pebbles. Wilma is a homemaker who is known for her kind heart and sharp wit. She is often the voice of reason in the Flintstone household.

Barney Rubble

Barney Rubble is Fred's best friend and neighbor. He is a loyal and caring husband to Betty Rubble and father to Bamm-Bamm. Barney is known for his playful nature and willingness to help his friends.

Betty Rubble

Betty Rubble is Barney's wife and mother of Bamm-Bamm. She is a kind-hearted and supportive friend to Wilma Flintstone. Betty is known for her sweet nature and loyalty to her loved ones.

Pebbles Flintstone

Pebbles Flintstone is Fred and Wilma's daughter. She is a cute and curious baby who often gets into mischief. Pebbles is known for her signature hairstyle, which consists of a bone-shaped hair clip.

Bamm-Bamm Rubble

Bamm-Bamm Rubble is Barney and Betty's son. He is a strong and energetic toddler who has a habit of hitting things with his club. Bamm-Bamm is known for his catchphrase "Bamm-Bamm!"

Dino the Dog

Dino the Dog is the Flintstones' pet dinosaur. He is a lovable and playful companion who often gets into humorous situations. Dino is known for his loyalty and his ability to express emotions like a human.
